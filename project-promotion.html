Cannon Climbing


Cannon Climbing is a thrilling precision platformer where you play as a cannon shooting your way to new heights. Ascend a massive test chamber filled with unique obstacles and Outmaneuver laser-wielding robots along the way. Use your knockback to gain momentum and reach new heights, but beware - one wrong move and you'll be sent back to the bottom. Can you conquer the ultimate test of skill and make it to the top? Wishlist now and find out!



Features:


  • Play as a cannon and use shooting to propel yourself to new heights
  • Ascend a massive test chamber filled with different themed levels
  • Dodge enemy robots and their laser attacks
  • Use knockback from shooting to gain momentum and reach new heights
  • Challenging gameplay that tests your skills
  • Multiple achievements to unlock
  • Steam Leaderboards to compete with other players
  • Connect to twitch
