Yukimura


Trapped in a remote, snowbound village, uncover the haunting secrets buried beneath Yukimura's endless winter. A survival horror experience inspired by Japanese folklore and ancient traditions.

  • Explore a handcrafted, snow-covered village inspired by gassho-zukuri architecture.
  • Uncover hidden folklore through artifacts, documents, and encounters.
  • Manage your warmth and resources carefully — survival is not guaranteed.
  • Face supernatural threats that grow stronger as the village consumes you.
  • Immerse yourself in a world of loneliness, beauty, and dread.
